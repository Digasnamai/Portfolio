Milagre Metaleiro (Metal Miracle)

About

This project entailed rebranding Milagre Metaleiro, an independent metal festival located in Pindelho dos Milagres, Portugal. The goal was to work along the wordplay contrast between milagre (miracle) and metaleiro (metal) within a coherent brand identity.

I created the main concept, the skull illustrations, the "Metaleiro" typography altered fom a blackletter font, and UI/UX design for the website as well as the functioning wireframe in figma.

Project Info

Roles: Illustration, Development, Art direction

Team Size: 4 people

Time frame: 8 weeks

Year: 2024
